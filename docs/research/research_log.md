# HealthPass Research Log

## Project Plan/Vision
HealthPass is an intelligent patient-controlled digital health passport that aims to help patients organise, understand and communicate their fragmented healthcare information. HealthPass uses explainable AI and pattern recognition to improve communication between patients and healthcare professionals.

---
## Problem Statement
Healthcare information tends to be fragmented across multiple providers, making it difficult for patients to understand and communicte their own health history when needed. Existing systems often store information but do little to help patients interpret it or identify meaningful long-term trends. 

---
## Research Question (Options - can decide which one later)
1. How can an intelligent patient-controlled digital health passport improve communication between patients and healthcare professionals through automated document analysis and pattern recognition?
2. How can explainable artificial intelligence improve the organisation, interpretation, and communication of fragmented patient health records within a patient-controlled digital health passport?
3. How can pattern recognition and natural language processing be used to identify and present meaningful trends within fragmented patient health records?
4. How can software engineering techniques be used to improve the accessibility and communication of complex healthcare information for patients?
