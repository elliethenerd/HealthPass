## MyCare.scot

### Purpose
The primary purpose of MyCare.scot is to improve how patients interact with NHS Scotland by providing a secure digital gateway to healthcare information and services. It aims to provide access to personal health records while supporting the wider digital transformation of healthcare across Scotland.

### Target users
- Patients registered within NHS Scotland
- Individuals accessing healh and social care services
- Healthcare professionals supporting patient care
- People who want convienent digital access to their healthcare information

### Key features
- Access hospital appointments and clinical information
- View hospital letters
- Check medication and allergy records
- Access vaccination history
- View personal NHS information, including CHI details
- Secure login using ScotAccount

### Strengths
- Backed by NHS Scotland
- Improves accessibility by reducing reliance on paper documents.
- Places a strong emphasis on privacy, security and patient control of personal information.

### Weaknesses
- Focuses primarily on providing access to healthcare information rather than helping patients understand it.
- Offers limited interpretation or explanation of complex medical information.
- Does not identify trends or recurring patterns across a patient's medical history.
- Patients cannot easily combine records from multiple healthcare providers into one complete health record.
- Lacks intelligent features such as AI-generated summaries, personalised timelines or consultation preparation tools.

### What HealthPass can learn
- Prioritise strong security and patient privacy from the beginning of development.
- Design a clean, accessible interface that is easy for patients of all technical abilities to use.
- Ensure compatibility with existing healthcare services instead of attempting to replace them.
- Give users clear control over how their personal health information is shared and managed.

### Gap HealthPass fills
MyCare.scot demonstrates the value of providing patients with secure digital access to healthcare information, but its primary role is to present existing records rather than help patients interpret them. HealthPass builds upon this idea by focusing on understanding and communication. Through features such as explainable AI summaries, the Health Story, Pattern Finder and consultation briefs, HealthPass aims to help patients make sense of fragmented medical information and communicate their healthcare journey more effectively with healthcare professionals.
