# Design Decisions

## DD-001

Decision

HealthPass will not diagnose medical conditions.

Reason

The project focuses on improving communication rather than replacing healthcare professionals.

---

## DD-002

Decision

Patients own and control their healthcare information. When sharing they will be able to choose what is public.

Reason

Improves privacy, trust and GDPR compliance.

---

## DD-003

Decision

AI outputs must always provide evidence.

Reason

Healthcare software should be transparent and explainable.

---

## DD-004

Decision

Pattern Finder provides observations rather than conclusions.

Reason

Avoids misleading users while still providing useful insights.
